Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At HAKILABZ, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our applications.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways, including:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, and other voluntarily submitted contact information.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time on site, referring URLs, and browser type — collected via analytics tools.
  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our website and applications.
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests.
  • Understand usage patterns to enhance the user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how visitors interact with our site. We use Google Analytics with IP anonymisation enabled. Analytics are only activated after you provide cookie consent via our banner.

You may decline cookies through the cookie consent banner. You can also control cookies through your browser settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except when required by law or to trusted partners who assist us in operating our website and services (such as analytics providers), subject to confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@hakilabz.ai.
