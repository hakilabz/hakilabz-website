Privacy Policy

Party GameHub · Last Updated: April 4, 2026 · Effective Date: April 4, 2026

Hakilabz ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Party GameHub mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Player Names: When setting up a game session, you enter player names. These names are stored locally on your device only and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Age Verification: We ask whether you are 18 years or older to determine content appropriateness. This check occurs each time the App is launched. We do not collect or store your date of birth.
  • Game Preferences: Settings such as theme preference (dark/light mode), sound effects, haptic feedback, strikes system toggle, and content spice level preferences are stored locally on your device.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Game Statistics: We store gameplay statistics (games played, scores, play time, awards, strikes) locally on your device using on-device storage (AsyncStorage). This data is not transmitted to any external server.
  • Device Information: Our third-party advertising partners may collect device identifiers and technical information for ad delivery purposes (see Section 3).
  • Purchase Information: When you subscribe to Party GameHub PRO, purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.
  • Crash Reports: We collect anonymized crash reports through Firebase Crashlytics to improve App stability. These reports contain technical device information and stack traces but no personal data.

1.3 Multiplayer Data

  • LAN Mode: Game data is transmitted directly between devices on the same local Wi-Fi network using peer-to-peer connectivity (Bonjour/mDNS). No data leaves your local network.
  • Online Mode: Game state is relayed through our WebSocket server (hosted on Cloudflare Workers) to enable cross-network play. Only game state data (player names, scores, game actions) is transmitted. Messages are ephemeral and are not stored on the server after relay. Room codes expire after the session ends.

1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Precise location data
  • Photos or media files
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Health or biometric data
  • Social media profiles

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide the App: Deliver game content appropriate to your selected spice level and age group.
  • Store Preferences: Remember your settings (theme, sound, haptics, strikes, content level) locally on your device.
  • Track Game Statistics: Maintain local gameplay history, scores, and award achievements on your device.
  • Display Advertisements: Show ads to free-tier users through Google AdMob (see Section 3). Production ad unit IDs are used in release builds; test ads are shown in development builds.
  • Process Subscriptions: Facilitate PRO subscription purchases and restore purchases through App Store or Google Play.
  • Enable Multiplayer: Facilitate real-time game sessions between devices over local network or online relay.
  • Improve Stability: Use anonymized crash reports to identify and fix bugs.
  • Version-Aware Updates: Show onboarding or update information screens when the App version changes.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements to free-tier users. Ad formats include interstitial ads (between games), app-open ads (on launch), and native inline ads (during gameplay). AdMob may collect and use certain information to deliver personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address
  • Device type, operating system, and version
  • App usage data related to ad interaction

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

Ad Tracking: On iOS, we request your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before accessing your device's advertising identifier for personalized ads. You can opt out at any time through your device settings.

PRO Subscribers: If you subscribe to Party GameHub PRO, advertisements are disabled entirely and no ad-related data is collected.

3.2 Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics

We use Firebase Analytics for anonymized app usage analytics and Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting. These services collect:

  • Anonymized usage patterns (screens viewed, features used)
  • Crash stack traces and device technical information
  • App performance metrics

No personally identifiable information is collected through these services.

3.3 Apple App Store / Google Play Store

Subscription purchases, payment processing, and purchase restoration are handled entirely by Apple or Google. We do not have access to your payment details (credit card numbers, bank accounts, etc.). Please refer to:

  • Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Google's Privacy Policy

3.4 Local Network (LAN Multiplayer)

When using the LAN multiplayer feature, the App uses Apple's Bonjour protocol (iOS) or Network Service Discovery (Android) to discover nearby game sessions on your local Wi-Fi network. Game data is transmitted directly between devices on the same network. No data passes through our servers.

3.5 Online Multiplayer Relay

When using online multiplayer, game state data is relayed through our server infrastructure (Cloudflare Workers). No personal data is stored on the server. Sessions are ephemeral and all data is discarded when the game session ends.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All user data (player names, game statistics, preferences, subscription entitlements) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
  • No User Accounts: The App does not require account creation, login, or authentication.
  • No Persistent Server Storage: We do not store personal data on remote servers. Online multiplayer relay data is ephemeral.
  • Purchase Restoration: Since there are no user accounts, subscription entitlements are tied to your Apple ID or Google Play account and can be restored on any device using the same account.
  • Security: We implement reasonable measures to protect the information processed by the App.

5. Children's Privacy

Party GameHub is intended for users aged 13 and older. The App includes an age verification gate that appears on every launch. Users who indicate they are under 18 receive family-friendly content only (Spice Levels 0-1). Users 18 and older may access mature content categories.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The App does not require any personal information to function. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking; Android: Settings > Google > Ads).
  • Delete Local Data: You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data from your device.
  • Subscription Management: You can manage or cancel your PRO subscription through your App Store or Google Play subscription settings at any time.
  • Restore Purchases: You can restore your PRO subscription on a new device using the same Apple ID or Google Play account.
  • Content Preferences: You can adjust your spice level and content preferences at any time within the App settings.
  • Sound and Haptics: You can enable or disable sound effects and haptic feedback independently within the App settings.
  • Strikes System: You can enable or disable the strikes penalty system within the App settings.

7. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Player names, settings, game statistics, and preferences are stored on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data. Data retention is entirely under your control.
  • Multiplayer Data: Online game sessions are ephemeral. No game data is retained on our relay servers after the session ends.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymized crash reports are retained by Firebase Crashlytics for up to 90 days for stability analysis.

8. International Users

The App is available internationally. By using the App, you understand that your information may be processed by third-party services (Google AdMob, Firebase) in accordance with their respective privacy policies and applicable data protection laws, including GDPR for users in the European Economic Area and CCPA for users in California.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. The App may display update information screens when the version changes. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when they are posted on this page.
