Party GameHub

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 8, 2026 · Effective Date: March 8, 2026

Hakilabz ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Party GameHub mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Player Names: When setting up a game session, you enter player names. These names are stored locally on your device only and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Age Verification: We ask whether you are 18 years or older to determine content appropriateness. We do not collect or store your date of birth.
  • Game Preferences: Settings such as theme preference (dark/light mode), sound, haptics, and content preferences are stored locally on your device.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Game Statistics: We store gameplay statistics (games played, scores, play time, awards) locally on your device using on-device storage. This data is not transmitted to any external server.
  • Device Information: Our third-party advertising partners may collect device identifiers and technical information for ad delivery purposes (see Section 3).
  • Purchase Information: When you subscribe to Party GameHub PRO, purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.
  • Analytics Data: We collect anonymized app usage data through Firebase Analytics, including screen views, game events, and general device information (device model, OS version, app version). This data is used to improve the App and does not include personal information.
  • Crash Reports: We collect crash and error reports through Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix bugs. These reports include device state and error details but no personal information.

1.3 Multiplayer Data

  • LAN Mode: Game data is transmitted directly between devices on the same local Wi-Fi network using peer-to-peer connectivity. No data leaves your local network.
  • Online Mode: Game state is relayed through our WebSocket server to enable cross-network play. Only game state data (player names, scores, game actions) is transmitted. Messages are ephemeral and are not stored on the server after relay.

1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Precise location data
  • Photos or media files
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Health or biometric data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide the App: Deliver game content appropriate to your selected spice level and age group.
  • Store Preferences: Remember your settings (theme, sound, content level) locally on your device.
  • Track Game Statistics: Maintain local gameplay history and award achievements on your device.
  • Display Advertisements: Show ads to free-tier users through Google AdMob (see Section 3).
  • Process Subscriptions: Facilitate PRO subscription purchases through App Store or Google Play.
  • Enable Multiplayer: Facilitate real-time game sessions between devices over local network or online relay.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements to free-tier users. AdMob may collect and use certain information to deliver personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address
  • Device type, operating system, and version
  • App usage data related to ad interaction

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

Ad Tracking: On iOS, we request your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before accessing your device's advertising identifier for personalized ads. You can opt out at any time through your device settings.

PRO Subscribers: If you subscribe to Party GameHub PRO, advertisements are disabled entirely and no ad-related data is collected.

3.2 Apple App Store / Google Play Store

Subscription purchases and payment processing are handled entirely by Apple or Google. We do not have access to your payment details (credit card numbers, bank accounts, etc.). Please refer to:

  • Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Google's Privacy Policy

3.3 Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics

We use Google Firebase to improve App stability and understand how the App is used:

  • Firebase Analytics collects anonymized usage data including screen views, game events (game started, completed), in-app purchase events, and general device information (device model, OS version, app version). This data helps us understand which features are popular and improve the App experience. Firebase Analytics does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or precise location.
  • Firebase Crashlytics automatically collects crash reports and non-fatal error logs to help us identify and fix bugs. Crash reports include device state, OS version, app version, and a stack trace of the error. No personal information is included in crash reports.

For more information, see Firebase Privacy Information and Google's Privacy Policy.

3.4 Local Network (Multiplayer)

When using the LAN multiplayer feature, the App uses Apple's Bonjour protocol to discover nearby game sessions on your local Wi-Fi network. Game data is transmitted directly between devices on the same network. No data passes through our servers.

3.5 Online Multiplayer Relay

When using online multiplayer, game state data is relayed through our server infrastructure. No personal data is stored on the server. Sessions are ephemeral and all data is discarded when the game session ends.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All user data (player names, game statistics, preferences, entitlements) is stored locally on your device using on-device storage.
  • No User Accounts: The App does not require account creation, login, or authentication.
  • No Persistent Server Storage: We do not store personal data on remote servers. Online multiplayer relay data is ephemeral.
  • Security: We implement reasonable measures to protect the information processed by the App.

5. Children's Privacy

Party GameHub is intended for users aged 13 and older. The App includes an age gate during initial setup that asks users whether they are 18 or older. Users who indicate they are under 18 are automatically restricted to family-friendly content only (Spice Levels 0–1: Corporate and Family). Users 18 and older may access all content categories including mature content.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The App does not require account creation, login, or any personal information to function. If you believe a child under 13 is using the App, we encourage their parent or guardian to supervise their use and select appropriate content settings. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking; Android: Settings > Google > Ads).
  • Delete Local Data: You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data from your device.
  • Subscription Management: You can manage or cancel your PRO subscription through your App Store or Google Play subscription settings at any time.
  • Content Preferences: You can adjust your spice level and content preferences at any time within the App settings.

7. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Player names, settings, and game statistics are stored on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data. Data retention is entirely under your control.
  • Multiplayer Data: Online game sessions are ephemeral. No game data is retained on our relay servers after the session ends.

8. International Users

The App is available internationally. By using the App, you understand that your information may be processed by third-party advertising services (Google AdMob) in accordance with their respective privacy policies and applicable data protection laws, including GDPR for users in the European Economic Area.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when they are posted on this page.

10. Responsible Drinking Disclaimer

Party GameHub includes optional drinking game mechanics for users 18 and older. The App is for entertainment purposes only. We do not encourage or promote excessive alcohol consumption. Please drink responsibly. Never drink and drive. Know your limits.
