Privacy Policy

Party GameHub · Last Updated: March 8, 2026 · Effective Date: March 8, 2026

Hakilabz ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Party GameHub mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Player Names: Entered for game sessions. Stored locally on your device only — never transmitted to our servers.
  • Age Verification: We ask whether you are 18+ to determine content appropriateness. We do not collect or store your date of birth.
  • Game Preferences: Settings such as theme, sound, haptics, and content preferences are stored locally on your device.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Game Statistics: Stored locally on your device. Not transmitted to any external server.
  • Device Information: Our third-party advertising partners may collect device identifiers for ad delivery (see Section 3).
  • Purchase Information: Transactions processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not collect or store payment information.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymised usage data via Firebase Analytics (screen views, game events, device info). No personal information.
  • Crash Reports: Via Firebase Crashlytics for bug identification. Includes device state and error details, no personal information.

1.3 Multiplayer Data

  • LAN Mode: Data transmitted directly between devices on the same Wi-Fi network. No data leaves your local network.
  • Online Mode: Game state relayed through our WebSocket server. Only game state data is transmitted. Messages are ephemeral — not stored after relay.

1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Precise location data
  • Photos, media files, or contacts
  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Health or biometric data

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the App: Deliver game content appropriate to your selected spice level and age group.
  • Store Preferences: Remember your settings locally on your device.
  • Track Game Statistics: Maintain local gameplay history and award achievements on your device.
  • Display Advertisements: Show ads to free-tier users through Google AdMob (see Section 3).
  • Process Subscriptions: Facilitate PRO subscription purchases through App Store or Google Play.
  • Enable Multiplayer: Facilitate real-time game sessions over local network or online relay.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements to free-tier users. AdMob may collect device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA), IP address, device type, OS, and app usage data related to ad interaction. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Ad Tracking (iOS): We request permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework before accessing your advertising identifier. You can opt out at any time through device settings.

PRO Subscribers: Advertisements are disabled entirely — no ad-related data is collected.

3.2 Apple App Store / Google Play Store

All payment processing handled by Apple or Google. We have no access to your payment details. See Apple's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy.

3.3 Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics

Firebase Analytics collects anonymised usage data (screen views, game events, device info) to improve the App. No personal information. Firebase Crashlytics collects crash reports (device state, OS version, stack traces) — no personal information. See Firebase Privacy Information.

3.4 Local Network & Online Multiplayer

LAN multiplayer uses Apple's Bonjour protocol. Data stays on your local network. Online multiplayer sessions are ephemeral — all data discarded when the game ends.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All user data (player names, stats, preferences) stored locally on your device.
  • No User Accounts: The App requires no account creation, login, or authentication.
  • No Persistent Server Storage: We do not store personal data on remote servers.
  • Security: We implement reasonable technical measures to protect information processed by the App.

5. Children's Privacy

Party GameHub is intended for users aged 13 and older. Users who indicate they are under 18 are automatically restricted to family-friendly content (Spice Levels 0–1). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Ad Personalisation: Opt out via device settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking; Android: Settings > Google > Ads).
  • Delete Local Data: Uninstall the App or clear app data from your device.
  • Subscription Management: Manage or cancel PRO through your App Store or Google Play settings.

7. Data Retention

Local data is retained until you uninstall the App or clear app data. Online multiplayer data is ephemeral — not retained after sessions end.

8. International Users

The App is available internationally. Third-party advertising services (Google AdMob) process information in accordance with their policies and applicable data protection laws including GDPR.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective when posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Responsible Drinking Disclaimer

Party GameHub includes optional drinking game mechanics for users 18+. The App is for entertainment purposes only. We do not encourage excessive alcohol consumption. Please drink responsibly and never drink and drive.
